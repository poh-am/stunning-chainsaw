## Purpose
Provide Scrum Masters with a standardized checklist for running effective sprint ceremonies.

## Sprint Planning Checklist
- [ ] Product backlog refined and prioritized before planning
- [ ] Sprint goal defined by Product Manager
- [ ] Team capacity calculated (accounting for PTO, meetings, etc.)
- [ ] User stories have clear acceptance criteria
- [ ] Stories estimated and sized appropriately
- [ ] Sprint backlog agreed upon by the team
- [ ] Dependencies identified and communicated
- [ ] Sprint board updated with committed items

## Daily Standup Checklist
- [ ] Meeting timeboxed to 15 minutes
- [ ] Each team member shares: progress, plans, blockers
- [ ] Blockers documented and assigned for follow-up
- [ ] Board updated to reflect current status
- [ ] Follow-up discussions scheduled separately (parking lot)

## Sprint Review (Demo) Checklist
- [ ] Stakeholders invited and confirmed
- [ ] Demo environment prepared and tested
- [ ] Demo script or agenda prepared
- [ ] Completed stories ready for demonstration
- [ ] Feedback captured and documented
- [ ] Action items assigned if needed

## Sprint Retrospective Checklist
- [ ] Meeting timeboxed (45-75 minutes)
- [ ] Anonymous input method available if needed
- [ ] Review previous action items and their status
- [ ] Collect feedback: What went well? What could improve?
- [ ] Prioritize top 2-3 improvement items
- [ ] Assign owners and due dates for action items
- [ ] Document outcomes and share with team

## Backlog Refinement Checklist
- [ ] Upcoming sprint items reviewed
- [ ] Acceptance criteria clarified
- [ ] Estimates updated or added
- [ ] Technical questions addressed with Developers
- [ ] Dependencies identified
- [ ] Large items broken down if needed
